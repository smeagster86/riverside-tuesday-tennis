Weekly Highlights
League Rules & Format
Questions? Message the organizer
Schedule
Every Tuesday evening, May 4 – end of September 2026 (~22 weeks). No drop weeks counted at end of season.
Court Grouping & Partners
Players grouped on courts (usually 4 per court, sometimes 5). Within each court you rotate partners — play with every other player on the court exactly once.
Match Format
4-player courts: 3 sets, first to 6 games (ad scoring, tiebreak at 5-5). 5-player courts: 5 sets, first to 5 (tiebreak at 4-4).
Weekly Points (How Standings Are Built)
N = number of players who actually played (have scores written on sheet). Players ranked by total games won that night across their sets. Top player gets N points, next N-1, down to 1. Court blocks are consecutive (top court = highest points).
Court Movement
Highest scorer on a court moves up one court next week. Lowest scorer moves down. This creates natural skill progression throughout the season.
Final Standings
At the end of the season your official total = sum of all weeks minus your two lowest scoring weeks (zeros from missed nights count as low weeks).
